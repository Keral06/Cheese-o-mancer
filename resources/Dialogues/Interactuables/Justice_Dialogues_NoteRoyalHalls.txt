Notice from the Royal Halls:
“The most recent batch of wheat shall be
milled and mixed with sawdust to feed the hungry below.
It is to be separated from the previous harvests so as not to let the
weevils spread to the noblefolk’s supply.
It shall also be reminded TONY that any who are caught
keeping any of the dwindling white flour TONY will suffer
the appropriate punishment.
Tony, you do not have more than three fingers by this point”
